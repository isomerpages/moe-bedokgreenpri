<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>A scientist in every student.</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<h4>Inquire</h4>
<p>Develop students who think and act like scientists by asking questions,
investigating ideas, and making sense of evidence with curiosity and confidence.</p>
<h4>Innovate</h4>
<p>Enable students to apply scientific knowledge creatively to real-world
contexts, generating solutions through critical and inventive thinking.</p>
<h4>Ignite</h4>
<p>Foster joy and motivation in learning Science, helping students make connections
between everyday phenomena and scientific ideas.</p>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<h4>Excellence 2000 (E2K) Science</h4>
<p>A two-year enrichment programme for selected Primary 4 and 5 students
with strong interest and aptitude in Science. Students develop scientific
inquiry skills and key 21st-century competencies through hands-on investigations
and open-ended challenges.</p>

<h4>Science Busking</h4>
<p>Students take part in Science Busking organised by the Science Centre
Singapore, where they design and conduct live, hands-on demonstrations
to explain scientific concepts in engaging and interactive ways. Through
this authentic experience, students deepen their conceptual understanding,
develop confidence in public speaking, and strengthen their communication
skills as they make science accessible and meaningful to diverse audiences.</p>

<h4>e-THINK Challenge</h4>
<p>Students take part in the e-THINK Challenge, organised by Temasek Junior
College, where Primary 5 and 6 learners apply creative thinking and scientific
knowledge to solve real-world problems. Using themed challenges and video
submissions, students communicate their understanding of concepts such
as thermal physics and simple machines, while strengthening inquiry skills
and 21st-century competencies.</p>

<h3>Authentic and Experiential Learning</h3>
<p>Our Science programmes emphasise hands-on, minds-on learning, where students
learn through inquiry, design, and reflection, making meaningful connections
between science and everyday life.</p>
<h4>Sailboat Challenge</h4>
<p>Students design and test sailboats to apply their understanding of forces
and motion, refining their designs through experimentation and iteration.</p>

<h4>Class Sunflower Project</h4>
<p>Students grow their own sunflowers to learn about plant parts and growth.
Friendly inter-class competition motivates responsibility and sustained
care.</p>
