**3 Day Orientation Programme** <br>

BGPS starts the year with the 3 day orientation programme to celebrate students’ homecoming, familiarize students to the school premises, reinforce safety guidelines in school and inculcate school values and TLIM habits.&nbsp;

  

We also incorporated the Social Skills and Peer Support Programme for Primary School in the 3-day Orientation Programme starting 2019 whereby we train class committee members on social skills and peer support.
