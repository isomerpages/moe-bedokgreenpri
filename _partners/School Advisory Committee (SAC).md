<p>The SAC is made up of members that represents and reflects the diversity
of Singapore’s community. They play an active role in promoting excellence
in school by providing advice on strategic directions of the school based
on their expertise, diverse perspectives and experiences. They provide
the network to garner stakeholders and community support for the school
to achieve positive student learning outcomes. In addition, they help to
raise funds to contribute to the holistic development of students.</p>
